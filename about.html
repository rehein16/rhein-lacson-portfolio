I'm a 3rd-year B.S. Information Technology student at Holy Angel University in the Philippines, specializing in web development, UI/UX design, and database management. I care about building things that are fast, purposeful, and genuinely good-looking.

My journey started with curiosity — tinkering with HTML, breaking things, fixing them, and slowly getting better. Today I build full-page web experiences from scratch: from the first wireframe sketch to the deployed live site.

I believe great software is empathetic. Every click, scroll, and interaction represents a real human on the other side — and that responsibility shapes how I design and code.
